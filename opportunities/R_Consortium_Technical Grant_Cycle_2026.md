The R Consortium Technical Grant Cycle is OPEN! 🚀

We fund projects that provide broad impact and demonstrable value to the global R community. Whether it’s developing critical open-source tools, improving R infrastructure on different operating systems, or supporting community-led social initiatives, we support the people - like you! - making R better for everyone.

The R Consortium is currently accepting proposals for our first grant cycle of 2026. If you have an idea that can strengthen the technical infrastructure of the R ecosystem, get in touch!

📅 Don't wait! Applications Close on May 1

List of previously approved projects: https://r-consortium.org/all-projects/funded-projects.html

Review the full guidelines and download the proposal template here: https://r-consortium.org/all-projects/callforproposals.html
